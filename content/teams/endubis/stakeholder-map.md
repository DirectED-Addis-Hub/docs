**Goal:** Create a legitimate, low-cost justice layer for decentralized systems.

## Digital Traders & Entrepreneurs

- **Role:** Engage in online and decentralized commerce.
- **Risk:** Financial loss without recourse.
- **Technology role:** Accessible dispute resolution and enforcement.

## Traditional Justice Systems

- **Role:** Legal authority.
- **Limitation:** Slow, expensive, and geographically bound.
- **Technology role:** Complement, not replace, existing systems.

## Decentralized Platforms & Protocols

- **Role:** Enable peer-to-peer transactions.
- **Pain:** Lack of native enforcement mechanisms.
- **Technology role:** Embedded dispute resolution logic.

## Community Elders & Cultural Institutions  
*(Inspired by the Gadaa system)*

- **Role:** Consensus-based accountability and legitimacy.
- **Value:** Social trust and collective judgment.
- **Technology role:** Encode cultural principles into digital governance.

## Regulators & Policymakers

- **Role:** Oversight and legal alignment.
- **Concern:** Unaccountable governance models.
- **Technology role:** Transparent, auditable systems.

## Users & Communities

- **Role:** Participants and beneficiaries.
- **Technology role:** Fair, understandable justice processes.
