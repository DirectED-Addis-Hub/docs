# EthioOrigin

## Problem
Smallholder farmers produce high-quality goods but cannot prove origin or quality. This enables fraud, reduces trust, and forces farmers to sell at low commodity prices.

## MVP

### Scope
- Mint each harvest batch as a Cardano native asset
- Store origin and quality data in NFT metadata
- Enable QR-based consumer verification

### Cardano Integration
Cardano NFTs (CIP-25) store immutable origin data, while transaction metadata records supply-chain handovers.
