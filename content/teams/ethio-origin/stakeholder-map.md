**Goal:** Restore farmer identity and trust across the coffee value chain.

## Smallholder Farmers

- **Role:** Grow and harvest coffee using regenerative practices.
- **Risk:** Low income, price manipulation, loss of identity.
- **Technology role:** Immutable proof of origin and practice.

## Washing Stations

- **Role:** Aggregate, process, and grade coffee.
- **Power:** Control quality classification and data flow.
- **Technology role:** Transparent, tamper-proof intake records.

## Cooperatives & Unions

- **Role:** Represent farmers and negotiate sales.
- **Challenge:** Limited visibility into downstream pricing.
- **Technology role:** Shared access to verifiable supply-chain data.

## Exporters & International Buyers

- **Role:** Sell coffee to global markets.
- **Pain:** Difficulty proving authenticity and ethical sourcing.
- **Technology role:** Verifiable origin records tied to farmers.

## Consumers

- **Role:** Demand traceability and ethical sourcing.
- **Gap:** No direct connection to farmers.
- **Technology role:** Access to trustworthy origin stories.

## Local Institutions

- **Role:** Regulation and support.
- **Technology role:** Oversight through transparent data without heavy bureaucracy.
