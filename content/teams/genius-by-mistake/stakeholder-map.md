**Goal:** Restore trust and transparency across agricultural value chains.

## Farmers

- **Role:** Grow and harvest crops.
- **Risk:** Income loss, rejected goods, lack of bargaining power.
- **Technology role:** Verifiable proof of origin and handling.

## Aggregators & Traders

- **Role:** Collect and move produce.
- **Pain:** Disputes over quality and source.
- **Technology role:** Shared, tamper-resistant records.

## Exporters

- **Role:** Sell to international markets.
- **Pain:** Shipment rejection and compliance risk.
- **Technology role:** End-to-end traceability.

## Buyers & Importers

- **Role:** Demand quality and authenticity.
- **Gap:** Limited visibility into origin.
- **Technology role:** Trusted traceability and verification.

## Regulators & Certification Bodies

- **Role:** Standards enforcement.
- **Technology role:** Transparent auditing without heavy paperwork.

## Consumers

- **Role:** Trust the story behind products.
- **Technology role:** Access to credible origin information.
