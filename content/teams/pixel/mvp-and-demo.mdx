## Problem
Handloom weavers in Mercato rely on foot traffic and expensive rentals, limiting visibility and income. Buyers struggle to identify genuine artisans.

## MVP

### Scope
- eCommerce storefront for weavers
- Seller identity verification

### Cardano Integration
Cardano wallets are used as a decentralized identity layer via CIP-30 wallet connection, establishing trusted seller identities.
