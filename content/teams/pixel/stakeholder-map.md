**Goal:** Increase visibility, trust, and income stability for female weavers.

## Artisans / Weavers

- **Role:** Produce handmade garments and textiles.
- **Risk:** Exploitation, invisibility, unstable income.
- **Technology role:** Verified digital profiles, direct-to-buyer access.

## Buyers  
*(Local, tourist, and international)*

- **Pain:** Difficulty verifying authenticity and origin.
- **Technology role:** Transparent maker identity and trust signals.

## Middlemen / Shop Owners

- **Role:** Gatekeepers to customers.
- **Risk:** Capture disproportionate value.
- **Technology impact:** Reduced dependency via direct marketplaces.

## Market Authorities / Local Administration

- **Role:** Manage stalls and regulation.
- **Technology role:** Vendor visibility, formalization, reduced congestion.

## Emerging Leaders  
*(Digitally skilled youth, designers, women entrepreneurs)*

- **Role:** Onboarding, storytelling, platform adoption.
