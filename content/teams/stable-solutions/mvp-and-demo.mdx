# Stable Solutions

## Problem
Prescription fraud and lack of verification lead to unsafe medication use, forged prescriptions, and preventable health risks.

## MVP

### Scope
- Issue verifiable prescription proofs
- Validate prescriptions at pharmacies
- Prevent reuse or duplication

### Cardano Integration
Cardano smart contracts store prescription hashes and enforce verification rules, ensuring prescriptions cannot be tampered with.
