**Goal:** Enable reliable, compliant, and affordable payment access for freelancers.

## Freelancers

- **Role:** Provide skilled digital services to global clients.
- **Risk:** Delayed income, lost earnings, burnout.
- **Technology role:** Simple, predictable payout mechanisms.

## Global Clients & Platforms  
*(Upwork, Fiverr, direct clients)*

- **Role:** Source global talent.
- **Pain:** Complicated payment processes.
- **Technology role:** Clear, compliant payout rails.

## Local Banks & Financial Institutions

- **Role:** Regulate and process payments.
- **Constraint:** Regulatory and currency controls.
- **Technology role:** Integration layers that reduce friction.

## Regulators  
*(National Bank of Ethiopia, policy bodies)*

- **Role:** Financial oversight and compliance.
- **Concern:** Capital controls and fraud prevention.
- **Technology role:** Transparent, auditable systems.

## Informal Intermediaries

- **Role:** Temporary workarounds.
- **Risk:** Trust, fraud, and dependency.
- **Technology impact:** Reduce reliance by formalizing access.

## Families & Dependents

- **Role:** Rely on freelancer income.
- **Impact:** Household instability during payment delays.
