**Goal:** Support safe and verifiable access to prescription medication.

## Patients

- **Role:** Seek relief from illness or discomfort.
- **Risk:** Harm from incorrect or unverified medication use.
- **Technology role:** Clear, trusted proof of valid prescriptions.

## Healthcare Providers  
*(Doctors, clinics, hospitals)*

- **Role:** Diagnose and prescribe medication.
- **Pain:** Prescriptions lose control once issued on paper.
- **Technology role:** Secure, verifiable prescription issuance.

## Pharmacies

- **Role:** Dispense medication.
- **Constraint:** No reliable way to verify authenticity of paper prescriptions.
- **Technology role:** Simple verification before dispensing.

## Regulatory Bodies

- **Role:** Ensure safe medication practices.
- **Challenge:** Enforcement without real time visibility.
- **Technology role:** Auditable and transparent prescription records.

## Families and Caregivers

- **Role:** Influence treatment decisions.
- **Risk:** Informal advice leading to misuse.
- **Technology role:** Clear guidance and reduced ambiguity.

## Technology Providers

- **Role:** Enable safe workflows.
- **Responsibility:** Avoid adding friction to critical health access.
