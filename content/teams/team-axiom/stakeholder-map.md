**Goal:** Enable trusted, verifiable patient referrals across health facilities.

## Patients

- **Role:** Seek timely medical care.
- **Risk:** Delayed or denied treatment due to paperwork issues.
- **Technology role:** Secure, portable referral records that protect privacy.

## Primary Health Centers

- **Role:** First point of care and referral initiation.
- **Pain:** Referrals questioned or rejected by hospitals.
- **Technology role:** Verifiable referral issuance with clear accountability.

## Hospitals / Secondary Facilities

- **Role:** Provide advanced care.
- **Pain:** Cannot easily verify external referrals.
- **Technology role:** Instant verification of referral authenticity.

## Healthcare Workers

- **Role:** Deliver care under pressure.
- **Risk:** Blamed for systemic failures.
- **Technology role:** Reduced administrative burden and dispute protection.

## Health Authorities & Regulators

- **Role:** Oversight and policy enforcement.
- **Technology role:** System-wide visibility without compromising patient privacy.

## Technology & Integration Partners

- **Role:** Infrastructure and interoperability.
- **Constraint:** Must work with low bandwidth and existing systems.
